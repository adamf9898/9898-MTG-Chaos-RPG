# Discussion Topics

Here are some suggested topics to start discussions for the project:

1. **Game Mechanics**
   - Share or get feedback on game design ideas and mechanics.

2. **Story Ideas**
   - Discuss storylines or lore to enrich the RPG experience.

3. **Technical Help**
   - Seek assistance with coding, design, or other technical aspects.

4. **Art and Assets**
   - Collaborate on art, music, or other creative assets.

5. **General Feedback**
   - Provide or request feedback on the game or any related topics.
